A Builder At Heart

I'm a second-year Computer Systems Engineer at the University of Georgia's Morehead Honors College, studying in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering.

As Chief Engineer of the MOCI mission at UGA's Small Satellite Research Laboratory, I lead hardware-software integration for a spacecraft destined for Low Earth Orbit — writing C-based flight software, architecting telemetry pipelines, and deploying machine learning at the edge.

Outside the lab I build whatever excites me — computer vision pipelines, custom embedded hardware, AI-powered apps, and polished web experiences. My philosophy: if it's worth building, it's worth doing right.
